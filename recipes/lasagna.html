Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, fennel seeds
  • Salt
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese: parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest then get stuck in!
